# Let's practice English grammar through Polish-to-English translation.

Give me one Polish sentence at a time that tests a specific grammar 
point (start with conditional sentences — zero, first, second, third, 
and mixed). Don't give me the next sentence until I've answered the 
current one.

After I translate it to English, check my answer and reply with:
1. My answer (as I wrote it)
2. The correct English version
3. A short explanation of the grammar point and why my answer was 
   right or wrong (note any specific word/vocab mistakes too)
4. My running score (correct/total)

Keep sentences varied in difficulty and increasingly test different 
conditional types as we go. If I ask, switch to a different grammar 
topic (e.g. reported speech, passive voice) using the same format.
